Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

An electronic voting system is a digital platform that allows eligible voters to cast their ballots online. Our system provides a secure, convenient alternative to traditional paper-based voting methods, enabling participation from any location with internet access.

Yes, our electronic voting system employs multiple layers of security, including end-to-end encryption, identity verification, and audit trails. In many ways, electronic voting can be more secure than traditional methods as it eliminates human errors in vote counting and prevents certain types of ballot tampering.

Our platform supports various types of elections, including general elections, local government elections, organizational elections, university student body elections, and corporate board elections. The system is flexible and can be configured for different voting methods, such as first-past-the-post, ranked-choice, or proportional representation.

Account & Security

To register, click on the "Register" button in the top navigation bar. You'll need to provide your full name, email address, and create a secure password. Depending on the election, you may also need to provide additional identity verification information.

Your personal information is encrypted and stored securely in compliance with data protection regulations. We implement strict access controls and follow industry best practices for data security. Your voting choices are stored separately from your personal information to maintain ballot secrecy.

If you forget your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. You'll be prompted to enter your email address, and we'll send you instructions for resetting your password. For security reasons, password reset links expire after 24 hours.

Voting Process

After logging in, navigate to the "Elections" page where you'll see a list of active elections you're eligible to participate in. Click on "Vote Now" for your desired election, make your selections on the ballot, review your choices, and then submit your vote by clicking "Cast Ballot."

No, once you've submitted your vote, it cannot be changed. This is consistent with traditional voting methods and ensures the integrity of the election. Please review your selections carefully before final submission.

After casting your vote, you'll receive a unique receipt code. You can use this code on the "Verify Vote" page to confirm your vote was included in the final tally. This verification system allows you to ensure your participation without revealing your specific voting choices.
